Stan Roach

WHEN I JOINED BROADWAY CHURCH:   November 2002

FAMILY LIFE:   We have two grown children, Brandon and Amanda. They have blessed us with four grandchildren, whom we dearly love spoiling!

SALVATION STORY:   I became a Christ-follower at a very young age (6 years old)! I really believe my early conversion was a direct result of living in a Godly home, and this has proven to be a driving factor in my passion for students and children. I made my first trip to the country of Belize in 2009 and fell head-over-heels in love with Belize and her people! Since 2009, Tammy and I have been going back to Belize every year, using Sports clinics as a vehicle to get into public schools and then sharing the gospel. In 2017, God laid it on our hearts to start a ministry called Belize Sports Outreach, where we lead teams every year to minister to hundreds of children throughout the country.

EDUCATION:   After high school, I spent 7 years in the US Navy. While in the Navy, I surrendered to full-time ministry and attended seminary at Mid-America Baptist Theological Seminary in Memphis, Tennessee. Since that time, it has been our joy to serve the Lord in several churches across the Mid-South area.

FUN FACT:   Some of my hobbies include duck hunting, riding motorcycles, camping and hanging out with my grandkids. I make a killer omelet, and I married my high school sweetheart over 35 years ago!
