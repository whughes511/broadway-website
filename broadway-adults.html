Bible Study

Small group Bible study classes are a place where you can connect with others, a special place where you can make new friends and feel like you belong. The classes will help you learn to study and apply God's Word to your life. Your faith will grow as the timeless truths of scripture are brought to life for you. Our Bible study classes will provide opportunities for you to serve the Lord with others, using your own spiritual gifts.

Care Groups

Care Groups are our method of creating a relational environment for spiritual growth. These groups meet in a non-church setting and vary by age, special needs, geographic location, family or life stages, etc. Every group is unique, but every group has the same purpose and is led by men and women with servants' heart. Our groups meet the second and fourth Sunday evening of each month, except during the summer months.

Young Adult Ministry

Our Young Adults ministry is specially designed to meet the needs of ages 18-25 in a casual and relational environment. This ministry exists to help believers grow in spiritual maturity and in a more intimate walk with the Lord and to find encouragement and support in navigating the ups and downs of life. This ministry also allows for opportunities to serve the Lord with God-given gifts and talents.

Senior Adult Ministry

The Senior Adult Ministry of Broadway Church (Ages 55 and older) seeks to provide opportunities for our more seasoned members to connect with God and to connect with other senior adults in order to grow in their Christian faith and to serve Christ with their special gifts.

Bible Study Leader

We are always looking for men and women to step into Bible Study leadership positions. Teaching the Word of God is at the forefront of our Adult Education ministry, and those with the spiritual gift of teaching are encouraged and taught how to use their gifts for God's glory in each class.

Care Group Leader

Do you love to host groups or teach the Word of God? We are looking for individuals to host care groups in homes on every second and fourth Sunday throughout the year. These leaders implant the Word of God into the lives of the family of God, cultivate vital relationships, and build up the Body of Christ.
