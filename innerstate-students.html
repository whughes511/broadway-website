Influence is not defined by age, so our desire is to equip students with a good understanding of who God is and who we are in Christ in order to change the world around us - whether that’s at school, on a team, at home, or even at church! We focus on training high school and middle school students to strengthen and live out their faith. We are unashamed of the Gospel in a world that is growing increasingly hostile toward the truth of God’s Word. We strive to cultivate students’ faith to permeate every part of their lives, accomplishing this through the power of the Holy Spirit and clear biblical teaching.

Romans 10:17 states, “So then faith [comes] by hearing, and hearing by the word of God,” hence, we teach teens to take personal ownership for their faith.

Sunday Mornings at 9:00am


Innerstate Student Ministry meets each Sunday morning at 9am for Bible study.

Sunday Nights at 5:00pm


Students in grades 6-12 get together for small group Bible study in the youth room or gym. The evening consists of a variety games, fantastic food, and relevant messages.

Wednesday Nights at 6:00pm


Innerstate Midweek is a weekly gathering on Wednesday Nights where we play games, worship Jesus, and then dive into our current midweek sermon series.
Often, we will break off and have specific Bible studies relevant to the students' grades,also known as HWY 678 (6-8 grade ministry), and HWY 912 (9-12 grade ministry).
