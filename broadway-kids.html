Volunteer
Our goal in Broadway Kids is to provide a safe place for kids to continue to grow their faith through interaction with God’s Word, committed Christian adults, and each other. Every game, activity, and lesson takes us back to the truth of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. We build on the foundations laid in our Kingdom Way Preschool, encouraging the Heart Transformation that will make our kids leaders in the next generation of Kingdom Expanders and Culture Shapers.

Sunday School

Our Broadway Kids Sunday school hour begins at 9am with music and games before transitioning to our small group time for age-graded interactive learning experiences through LifeWay’s Bible Studies for Life curriculum. Our teachers open up their Bibles and lead kids through God’s Word and how it applies to their lives. Check-in for Sunday school/Kids Church is at the Broadway Kids desk in the Welcome Center.

Broadway Kids Church

Our 10:15am worship hour is a fun, energetic, interactive large-group experience that builds on our Sunday school lesson. Through games, videos, and high-energy speakers, we deliver Biblical content that kids can understand and respond to. Kids Church takes place in room 202 on the north side of the building.

Pure Joy Ministries

Pure Joy is a ministry designed to meet the unique needs of children grades 1-5. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to hear the gospel in a way that they can understand and then to respond, and we can do that by focusing on a child’s abilities rather than their disabilities. Our heart is to minister not only to the child, but to his/her family, as well. In order to better achieve that goal, we need to know a few things about you and your child’s unique needs.

Adventure Time

Two Sunday nights a month we have a special Adventure Time! We engage the Scripture in fun and unique ways, eat dinner together, and hit the gym for some much-needed energy release. During the school year, join us every Sunday at 5:00pm.


UPCOMING EVENTS

Sunday School Leader

We believe that corporate worship is important, but Sunday school is where kids make connections: with Jesus and each other. That’s why being a Sunday school leader is the most important volunteer role we have—they share and live out Jesus for the 1-5 grade kids every week.

Pure Joy Volunteer

The Pure Joy Ministry is designed for our Broadway Kids with special needs. We want to offer them the same opportunity to meet Jesus that every other kid has, and then walk with them as they grow in their faith!

Welcome Team

These folks are the first people guests meet when they come through our doors! They assist with check-in, welcoming guests, and finding the right Sunday school classes for all different age groups.
