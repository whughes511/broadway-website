We're so excited to share In the Wild VBS with you this year at Broadway! Throw on your camera strap and buckle your seatbelt. VBS 2019 takes you on a wild adventure—with elephants and egrets, polar bears and penguins, cockatoos and crocodiles. As you seek out exotic animals, you’ll also find snapshots of real-life encounters with Jesus in Scripture. Get ready, wild encounters are on the way!
During this wild adventure, kids will get energized each morning during Worship Rally, enjoy learning about Jesus during Bible Study, have fun playing games during Recreation and - of course - quench their thirst during Snack Time! They’ll also get to spend time learning about how to help others across the world in Missions, get their creative juices flowing during Crafts and have lots of fun singing and dancing to the VBS songs during Music rotations. Come join us! It’s going to be a wild time!
Registration is now open! Kids having completed K-5th grades are invited to join us. You may complete your registration online or in person (during normal church hours) between now and June 1st.
