Tim Lampley

WHEN I JOINED BROADWAY CHURCH:   June 2004

FAMILY LIFE:   Married my lovely wife Lisa on July 2, 1983. We have three children. Our sons Seth and Nathan are in Heaven, and our daughter Beth Ann lives and works in Branson, MO.

SALVATION STORY:   I grew up in West Frankfort, Illinois with my parents and four sisters. I was the only boy, which made life interesting to say the least. My father was a construction worker and was gone much of the time. To help make ends meet, my mom opened a restaurant which consumed much of her time as well. The lack of supervision and the abundance of free time spelled trouble for me. By the age of fifteen, I was immersed in alcohol and drugs. My life was speeding out of control.

During that time, my best friend experienced a tremendous change in his life with God. He began to tell me how Jesus Christ died on the Cross for my sins and how He came back to life after three days. He shared that Jesus' death paid for everything wrong I had ever done and would do.

After a series of events, I confessed my faith in Jesus Christ on a warm afternoon in June of 1974. That wonderful day represents the turning point in my life. I became a brand new person.

It has been an incredible journey since that wonderful day. God has been with me every step of the way. Through good times and bad times, He has proven faithful. I cannot imagine where I would be without Jesus. Since 2006, it has been my joy to serve Broadway Baptist Church as their pastor. I hope you will come and worship with us very soon. I would love to have the opportunity to tell you more about God's overwhelming love.

EDUCATION:   Bachelor of Science, Texas Wesleyan University; Masters of Divinity, Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary.

FUN FACT:   I am a Harley Davidson enthusiast. I own a 2009 Road King. The call of the back roads provides tremendous enjoyment and relaxation.

tlampley@broadwaychurch.org

Stan Roach

WHEN I JOINED BROADWAY CHURCH:   November 2002

FAMILY LIFE:   We have two grown children, Brandon and Amanda. They have blessed us with four grandchildren, whom we dearly love spoiling!

SALVATION STORY:   I became a Christ-follower at a very young age (6 years old)! I really believe my early conversion was a direct result of living in a Godly home, and this has proven to be a driving factor in my passion for students and children. I made my first trip to the country of Belize in 2009 and fell head-over-heels in love with Belize and her people! Since 2009, Tammy and I have been going back to Belize every year, using Sports clinics as a vehicle to get into public schools and then sharing the gospel. In 2017, God laid it on our hearts to start a ministry called Belize Sports Outreach, where we lead teams every year to minister to hundreds of children throughout the country.

EDUCATION:   After high school, I spent 7 years in the US Navy. While in the Navy, I surrendered to full-time ministry and attended seminary at Mid-America Baptist Theological Seminary in Memphis, Tennessee. Since that time, it has been our joy to serve the Lord in several churches across the Mid-South area.

FUN FACT:   Some of my hobbies include duck hunting, riding motorcycles, camping and hanging out with my grandkids. I make a killer omelet, and I married my high school sweetheart over 35 years ago!

sroach@broadwaychurch.org

Carlene Belue

WHEN I JOINED BROADWAY CHURCH:   July 2006

FAMILY LIFE:   Married to my husband, Robert, since June of 1994. We have three kids: Caitlin, Cannon and Cash, who were lovingly and purposefully planned for us by God through the gift of adoption.

SALVATION STORY:   God graciously placed me in a family where I not only learned about Jesus, but saw Jesus lived out through the daily lives of my parents as they served in Guatemala. I lived there for 15 years, and my life was enriched through a different culture, learning a new language, and learning to trust God when life was hard. I accepted Christ at age 4, after listening to my dad read devotions to the elementary boys in the dorm where they were dorm parents at the school for missionary kids. I got up and went to my mom in the kitchen and told her I needed to ask Jesus into my heart, and she prayed with me. My dad baptized me at age 11 in the river near our home.

EDUCATION:   Bachelor of Science in Physical Education from The Master’s College, Secondary Teaching Credential in Physical Education, Master’s Degree in Educational Management and Administrative Services Credential from LaVerne University

FUN FACT:   I have climbed an active volcano. I can speak Spanish. I love to read. Our family has 6 horses, 1 donkey, 2 goats, 1 chicken, 1 dog, 3 cats, and 5 rabbits.
