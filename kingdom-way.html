Volunteer
Our desire is for our Babies-Kindergartners to learn about God and the truths of His Word in a loving, nurturing, and safe environment to which they love to return each week! Age-appropriate lessons, crafts and activities have, at their hearts, the goal of building a strong foundation for knowing and loving God. Even with our little ones, we can begin the life-long process of Heart Transformation and becoming Kingdom Expanders and Culture Shapers.

We have a check-in procedure with a tag for each parent/child for the safety of our kids.

Sunday Morning at 9:00am


Our Preschool ministry uses Lifeway’s Bible Studies for Life curriculum for our Babies-Kindergarten classes during our Bible Study hour. We have wonderful teachers who plan creative and age-appropriate lessons for each class.

Sunday Morning at 10:15am


We continue our preschool time with a snack, some playtime in our indoor “park” room, reinforcing the lesson learned at 9 am, and Worship for Life for our Pre-K and K classes.

Sunday Nights


  • On the 1st/3rd Sunday nights of each month, we meet from 5-6pm and review our Bible story from the morning along with a new craft or activity.

  • On the 2nd/4th Sunday nights, adults meet for Care Group in homes from 5-6:30pm, while all children/youth meet at the church for dinner and activities from 4:45-6:45pm.

Wednesday Night at 6pm


Our Wee Kids Praise program runs from Sept-May. Our little ones continue learning about Jesus and learn songs to sing on one Sunday of every month, to begin our main worship service. We also use this time to learn their monthly memory verse and receive another badge on their lanyard. During the summer months, we continue to study God’s Word with a variety of lessons and crafts.

Preschool Volunteers:

To volunteer in our Preschool Ministry, a person must be a church member for a minimum of 6 months, interview with the Preschool Director, and complete a volunteer application and background check.

We love our volunteers!

There are opportunities to serve on Sundays from 8:45-10am, 10-11:45am, and 5:45-7pm; and on Wednesdays from 5:45-7:15pm.
